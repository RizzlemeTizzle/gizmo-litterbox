# Ideal Customer: De Esthetisch Bewuste Kattenouder (NL/BE)

## Demographics
- **Age:** 28–45
- **Location:** Urban Netherlands & Belgium (Rotterdam, Amsterdam, Utrecht, Antwerp)
- **Profession:** Creative, knowledge worker, or professional (marketing, design, IT, healthcare, education)
- **Income:** Mid-to-high (comfortable discretionary spend)
- **Home:** Modern apartment or design-conscious house
- **Pets:** 1–2 cats treated as family

## Psychographics
- Obsessed with a tidy, visually cohesive interior
- Frustrated by ugly or leaking litter boxes
- Loves minimalism, simplicity, functional design
- Happy to invest in premium quality + convenience
- Cares about sustainability and reducing waste
- Prioritizes cat comfort, space, hygiene
- Relies on authentic reviews, real experiences, and smart solutions

## Motivations (direct quotes)
- “Ik wil een mooie kattenbak die niet stinkt.”
- “Mijn huis moet netjes en stijlvol blijven.”
- “Mijn kat verdient goede kwaliteit.”
- “Schoonmaken moet snel, makkelijk en hygiënisch zijn.”

## Pain points
- Leaky boxes ruining floors
- Persistent odor, damp pellets, mess
- Plastic eyesores that clash with interiors
- Boxes that are too small or unwelcoming
- Scatter and clutter around the litter area
- Cheap products that break quickly

## What convinces her?
- Lifestyle imagery pairing beautiful homes + relaxed cats
- Genuine customer reviews/testimonials
- Proof of odor-free, leak-free performance
- Simple explanation of the two-layer pellet system
- Emphasis on premium materials + durability
- Spacious, cat-friendly interior shots
- Calm, elegant tone in Dutch copy

## Brand cues for GIZMO
- **Minimalist**: clean layout, muted palette
- **Premium**: matte textures, high-end photography
- **Reliable**: straightforward benefits, no hype
- **Warm**: empathetic copy, human voice
- **Design-first**: showcase integration in stylish homes
- **Hygienic & smart**: highlight leak-proof engineering + easy cleaning
