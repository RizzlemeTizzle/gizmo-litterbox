# Wholesale & Distribution Playbook (Draft)

## Target partners
1. **Premium pet retailers (NL):** Pets Place XL, Ranzijn, Urban Pet Stores.
2. **Boutique interior/home concept stores** that stock stylish pet goods.
3. **Regional distributors** covering DE/FR/IT/SE for premium/luxury pet channels.

## Offer structure
- **MSRP:** €129.95–139.95 (country dependent).
- **Wholesale price:** 50–55% of MSRP depending on volume.
- **MOQ:** 40 units per SKU for initial order (negotiable).
- **Lead time:** 4–6 weeks ex-warehouse NL.
- **Marketing support:** lifestyle photography, UGC library, point-of-sale cards, quick assembly guide.

## Sales collateral checklist
- [ ] One-pager PDF (USPs, specs, hero photography, pricing tiers).
- [ ] Deck w/ market stats + testimonial quotes.
- [ ] Sample agreement + payment terms (30% deposit, 70% pre-shipment?).
- [ ] Logistics sheet (dimensions, HS codes, pallet configuration).

## Pipeline workflow
1. Build CRM sheet with columns: company, contact, channel, size, status, notes.
2. Weekly outreach target: 10 new retailers + 3 distributors.
3. Sequence: intro email → sample/demo offer → follow-up call → proposal.
4. Track in `wholesale/pipeline.csv` (TBD) + automate reminders.

## Open questions
- Finalize Incoterms (EXW vs. DAP) per region.
- Storage capacity + fulfillment partner for cross-border shipments.
- Country-specific certifications or packaging requirements.
